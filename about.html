Our mission

Why creos exists.

"We believe creators running commercial businesses deserve proper business tools — not PDFs, DMs, and spreadsheets."

The creator economy is enormous.

Millions of creators earn real income through brand deals, commercial photography, video production, and sponsored content. This is a serious industry — but the tools available treat it like a hobby.

The tools haven't caught up.

CRMs built for sales teams, invoicing tools built for accountants, media kits built for PR agencies. None of them fit the way a creator actually works. creos is built from the ground up for this exact use case.

We're fixing that.

creos is opinionated, focused software. It does one thing: helps creators who get paid to create run their businesses better. That's it. No feature bloat, no enterprise fluff.
